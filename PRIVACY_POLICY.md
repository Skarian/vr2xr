# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-22

`vr2xr` collects **0 user data**.

- No account creation or sign-in
- No analytics or tracking SDKs
- No advertising identifiers
- No collection, storage, or sale of personal information by the developer

If you open a video URL, the app connects only to that URL to play the video you requested. Any data handling by third-party websites, Google Play, or your device OS is governed by their own policies.

For privacy questions, open an issue in this repository.
